MedLab provides BC Citizens with a way to obtain a Digital Credential that certifies their non-infectious COVID-19 status. The MedLab issuer demonstrates a proof-of-concept digital credential issuer that might be used to provide residents with a Digital Credential that certifies their COVID-19 status. In order to access this service you will need to have obtained your (un)Verified Person Digital Credential. No personal information is be stored within the application and the COVID-19 status data issued is fictional. This website is NOT to be used for production purposes and is for demonstration only.
