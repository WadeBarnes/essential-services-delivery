The Health Gateway credential issuer demonstrates a proof-of-concept digital credential issuer that might be used to provide BC residents with a version of their Personal Health Number in the form of a Digital Verifiable Credential. In order to access this service you will need to have obtained your (un)Verified Person Digital Credential. No personal information is be stored within the application and the Personal Health Numbers issued are fictional. This website is NOT to be used for production purposes and is for demonstration only.
